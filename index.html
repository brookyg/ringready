Questions

Straight answers.

Will callers know they're talking to an AI?

RingReady is trained to handle calls naturally. Most callers don't know — and most don't care. What they care about is getting answered fast and getting booked. That's what RingReady does.

What if a call is too complex for the AI?

For calls outside your scope, RingReady takes a message, gets their details, and sends you an immediate alert so you can call back when you're free. No lead gets dropped.

Does it work with my current phone number?

Yes. You keep your number. RingReady picks up calls that you miss — via call forwarding. Setup takes about 2 minutes.

What calendar apps does it connect to?

Google Calendar and Apple iCal out of the box. More integrations coming. If you use a specific booking tool, get in touch and we'll sort it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No lock-in, no cancellation fees. We believe you'll stay because it works — not because we've trapped you.
